Common questions

How do I log a past trip?

Open the Timeline tab, long-press any date to enter selection mode, select the date range for your trip, then tap Add Log at the bottom of the screen.

Why does automatic tracking need background location?

Waymark checks your location once in the morning and once in the evening to determine which country you're in. It never tracks you continuously — only the inferred country code is stored, not your GPS coordinates.

How do I enable iCloud sync?

Go to Settings → iCloud in the app and toggle sync on. Your data will sync across all your devices signed into the same Apple ID.

How do I export my travel history?

Go to the Reports tab, choose your date range and format (CSV or PDF), and tap Export. Reports are generated entirely on your device.

How do I delete all my data?

Go to Settings → Your Data → Reset App. This permanently deletes all local records. If iCloud Sync was enabled, you can also remove the app's iCloud data from iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.

I'm having trouble with location permissions

If Waymark isn't capturing your location automatically, check that location access is set to Always in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Waymark. "While Using" mode won't allow background checks.
