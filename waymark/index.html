How it works

Automatic tracking

One location check in the morning, one in the evening. Waymark logs which country you started and ended your day in — no continuous GPS trail.

Residency goals

Set a minimum or maximum day target for any country. Waymark tracks your progress and alerts you when you're off pace.

Manual logging

Back-fill past trips in seconds. Select a date range, pick a country, done. Conflicts are handled gracefully.

Export-ready reports

Generate a daily log or summary and export as CSV or PDF — ready for your accountant, visa officer, or lawyer.

Your data stays on your device.

No account, no server, no subscription required. Raw GPS coordinates are never stored — only the inferred country code. Optionally sync across your own devices via iCloud.
